WHO ARE WE?

April Sourcing is a buying agency meeting the quality and delivery requirements of some of the most discerning stores worldwide.We are known for a unique product handwriting and the ability to continuously bring newness on the table. Design is our strength and we deliver across product categories.Over the course of the last two decades, April has come to be known not only for exceptional quality across all its product categories but also for the design solutions all its clients have access to. Headquartered in Gurgaon, Haryana, April is a family of 40 merchandisers, designers and quality controllers. Currently April provides supply chain and design solutions across home goods - hard and soft; apparel - knit, woven, embellished; hand bags - leather, embellished, fabric; accessories - hand bags, footwear and jewellery. April's list of clients in USA and UK include Anthropologie, Urban Outfitters, Free People, ASOS, Topshop, Oliver Bonas, Warehouse, New Look, Dorothy Perkins and House of Fraser.
